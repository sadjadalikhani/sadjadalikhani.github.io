We released the Large Wireless Model (LWM) arXiv preprint covering universal feature extraction for wireless channels (arXiv:2411.08872). The paper details the dataset curation process, pretraining strategy, and downstream transfer gains for channel sensing, beam prediction, and dataset similarity tasks.
